FAQ

Fast answers so you can book fast.

What areas do you serve?

Based in New York City and serving Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx. New Jersey and nearby areas are available by request.

How far in advance should I book?

Weekends book first. Aim for 2–4 weeks when you can. Smaller weekday events can sometimes fit with 7–10 days notice.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. Allergies and dietary needs are handled up front so the menu still hits. Add your needs in the booking form and I’ll confirm options.

What’s the minimum spend or guest count?

It depends on guest count, menu complexity, and service style. Share your headcount and budget range and I’ll reply with a realistic quote window.

How do brand collaborations work?

You send a brief, product details, and the goal. I’ll propose concepts, deliverables (video/photos/recipe), a timeline, and usage rights so everything is clean.

What’s included with catering?

Menu planning, sourcing, prep, and execution. Staffing, rentals, and service style are scoped per event so you know exactly what’s included.
