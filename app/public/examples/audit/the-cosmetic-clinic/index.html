Key Findings

Critical

Heavy Font Loading Blocks Rendering

Your site loads Cinzel and Gentium Book Basic — beautiful choices, but they're not optimized. No font-display: swap, no preload hints. Visitors see a blank page for 1–2 seconds while fonts download. On mobile, this pushes your Largest Contentful Paint past 4 seconds. Google penalizes this in search rankings.

Critical

No SSL Certificate Consistency

Some internal links point to HTTP instead of HTTPS. Mixed content warnings degrade trust signals. Google Chrome shows “Not Secure” on some page loads. For a medical practice handling appointment requests, this erodes patient confidence.

Warning

Gallery Images Missing Alt Text

Your treatment gallery has 20+ images with empty or generic alt attributes. Search engines can't index these images. You're missing visibility for searches like “Botox results Greenwich CT” or “dermal filler before after” — high-intent queries that drive bookings.

Opportunity

No Appointment Schema Markup

Adding MedicalBusiness and Service schema would help Google display your hours, services, and booking link directly in search results. Competitors with rich results get 20–30% higher click-through rates.
