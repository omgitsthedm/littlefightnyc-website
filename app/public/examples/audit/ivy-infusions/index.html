Analysis

Key Findings

Critical

Unoptimized Images Slowing Load Time

Your hero images and gallery load as full-resolution JPEG/PNG files with no WebP conversion, no lazy loading, and no srcset responsive attributes. This adds 3–4 seconds to your initial page load.

Visitors on mobile connections may leave before ever seeing your services. Clients searching for treatments on their phones are the most likely to bounce.

Estimated Monthly Impact

15–20% of mobile visitors bounce before the page finishes loading

Warning

Missing Local Business Schema

Your site lacks structured data markup—LocalBusiness, MedicalBusiness, and FAQPage schemas are all absent. Google and AI answer engines can’t properly categorize your services, hours, or location.

Competitors with schema markup appear in rich results. You don’t. This means fewer clicks from “med spa Greenwich CT” searches.

Warning

No FAQ Section for AI Search Engines

Perplexity, ChatGPT, and Google’s AI Overviews pull answers from well-structured FAQ content. Your site has no FAQ section.

When potential clients ask AI assistants “best med spa in Greenwich CT,” your site can’t be referenced. Adding 8–10 real questions with 2–3 sentence answers would make you visible to these platforms.

The Bottom Line

Revenue Impact

$3,000 – $5,000

Estimated Missed Revenue Per Month

Based on your market (Greenwich, CT med spa), an average client value of $800–$1,500 per visit, and estimated monthly site traffic, these issues are likely costing you thousands in missed appointments. Image optimization alone could recover 15–20% of mobile abandonment.

These are fixable. Most take less than a week.
