Key Findings

What's holding your site back from converting the patients searching for you right now.

Critical

Image-Heavy Pages Without Optimization

Your before/after galleries and treatment pages load full-resolution images without WebP conversion, lazy loading, or responsive sizing. On mobile, this means 8–12 MB of image data per page. Your Largest Contentful Paint exceeds 5 seconds. Google’s Core Web Vitals threshold is 2.5 seconds — you’re double that.

Warning

Missing FAQPage Schema for AI Visibility

When patients ask Perplexity or ChatGPT “best dermatologist Hinsdale IL for acne treatment,” your site can’t be referenced because there’s no structured FAQ content. Adding schema-marked FAQ sections for your top 10 treatments would make you visible to AI search platforms — the fastest-growing source of medical referrals.

Warning

CTA Buried Below the Fold

Your primary call-to-action (booking/contact) requires scrolling past the hero section. On mobile, the booking button doesn’t appear until 3+ scroll depths. Moving a sticky “Book Now” button or floating CTA to the viewport would capture visitors who are ready to act immediately.

Info

No Google Business Profile Integration

Your website doesn’t link to or embed your Google reviews. Displaying your Google rating and recent reviews on-site builds trust and keeps visitors from leaving to check reviews elsewhere — where they might discover competitors.
