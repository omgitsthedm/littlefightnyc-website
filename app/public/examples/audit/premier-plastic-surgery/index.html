Key Findings

Warning

Complex JavaScript Bundles

Your site loads multiple third-party scripts including analytics, chat widgets, and scheduling tools that add over 800KB of JavaScript. This blocks the main thread on mobile devices, causing 2–3 second interaction delays. Patients trying to book appointments on their phones experience unresponsive buttons and form fields, directly impacting your consultation booking rate.

Warning

No Procedure-Specific Landing Pages

Searches for terms like “rhinoplasty Summit NJ” or “facelift surgeon near me” should land on dedicated pages optimized for each procedure. Your current structure groups everything under general service categories. Each missing landing page is a missed opportunity to rank for high-value surgical procedure keywords in the competitive Summit and Short Hills market.

Info

Mobile Navigation Too Deep

Your mobile menu requires three or more taps to reach key pages such as booking, specific procedures, and insurance information. Best practice is two taps maximum. Simplifying the mobile navigation hierarchy would reduce drop-off and improve the patient scheduling flow, especially for first-time visitors researching procedures.
