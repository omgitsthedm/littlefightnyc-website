# Owner story scenes

These images support the reusable `OwnerStories` section. They are illustrative
New York business environments, not photographs of Little Fight clients and not
evidence of completed work.

## Scenes

- `neighborhood-barber.webp`: a familiar shop with chairs, mirrors, an
  appointment book, and a small booking screen
- `neighborhood-bistro.webp`: an intimate restaurant with set tables, a
  handwritten reservation book, and a small booking screen
- `family-law-office.webp`: a long-running office with legal books, files, an
  older desktop, and a newer laptop

Each scene has responsive 480, 640, 900, and 1200 pixel WebP variants. The
unmarked source is 1672 by 941 pixels.

## Usage

- Keep the visible caption `Illustrative scene. Not client evidence.` outside
  the image.
- Keep useful alt text that describes the room and business tools.
- Do not present these images inside a case study as client photography or
  project proof.
- Do not remove the responsive variants without updating
  `OwnerStories.tsx`.
