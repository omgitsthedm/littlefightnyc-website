# Little Fight NYC business environments

Generated for the Little Fight NYC brand system on 2026-07-24 with OpenAI's
built-in image generation tool.

These images are illustrative business environments. They are not client
locations, documentary evidence, testimonials, or case-study proof. Use them to
help an owner recognize a familiar setting while the surrounding copy explains
the service.

## Assets

- `restaurant-counter.webp` - restaurant counter with POS, printer, card reader,
  order tickets, and network equipment.
- `salon-systems.webp` - salon front desk and appointment systems before opening.
- `shop-back-office.webp` - retail stockroom workspace with paper, inventory,
  labels, and connected devices.
- `storefronts-dawn.webp` - neighborhood storefronts preparing to open.

Each scene also has `-480`, `-640`, `-900`, and `-1200` WebP variants for
responsive delivery. The unnumbered file is the 1672-pixel source used as the
largest fallback.

## Publishing rules

- Keep people absent, distant, or visually incidental.
- Do not add business names, client names, logos, customer data, or readable
  financial information.
- Do not caption these images as real client locations.
- Use concise environment-based alt text. Do not mention the image-generation
  process in public-facing alt text.
- Never use an illustrative environment as evidence for a performance,
  delivery-time, cost, or client-outcome claim.
