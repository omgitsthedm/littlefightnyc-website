## Color
| Role | Hex | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Lead Orange (signal) | `#F97316` | CTAs, accents, the "NYC" tag. **Use sparingly** — it's the signal. |
| Soft Orange | `#FB923C` | Hover, particles, secondary. |
| Midnight (bg) | `#06080F` | Primary background. |
| Panel | `#0E1220` / `#0A0D18` | Cards / elevated surfaces. |
| Blue Burst | `#2058AF` | Rare cool accent / data. Never competes with orange. |
| Ink | `#F0F2F8` | Primary text on dark. Muted text `#A1A1AA`. |

## Type
- **Display — Oswald 700** (condensed, all-caps): headlines + the wordmark. The loud voice.
- **Body — Barlow**: all reading text. Humanist, plain, honest.
- **Technical — JetBrains Mono**: metadata, code, eyebrows, technical values.

## Logo rules
- **Do:** clear space ≥ smokestack height; min size 20px (favicon) / 32px in the wild; mark in white/ink/orange only; "NYC" tag in orange.
- **Don't:** recolor the boat off-palette; stretch/rotate/gradient/shadow/outline it; set the wordmark in anything but Oswald 700.

## Voice
Plain. Confident. On the side of the little guy. We don't sell "solutions" — we **fix the tech slowing down your business**. No jargon, no fear, no corporate fog.
