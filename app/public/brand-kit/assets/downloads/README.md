# Little Fight NYC — Brand Kit

**The little guy who moves the big guy.**

A tugboat is small, stout, and unglamorous — and it pulls container ships ten
thousand times its size through the busiest harbor on earth. That's the whole
idea. Little Fight NYC is the tugboat for the small business. The mark isn't
decoration. It's the promise.

This kit is everything you need to put Little Fight NYC in front of the world and
never second-guess it. Live version: **https://littlefightnyc.com/brand-kit/**

---

## Start here

1. **Look** → open `little-fight-nyc-brand.html` in any browser. That's the visual
   brand book — the whole system on one beautiful, offline page.
2. **Read** → `BRAND-KIT.md` is the quick-reference spec (color, type, rules, voice).
3. **Build** → grab tokens from `color/palette.css` (CSS variables) or
   `color/tokens.json` (machine-readable, for design tools + code).

## The one-minute version

- **Mark** — the Tugboat. One color, `fill: currentColor`, reverses on any ground.
  Give it air (clear space ≥ the smokestack). Never recolor, stretch, or shadow it.
- **Color** — orange `#F97316` is the **signal** (use it like it's expensive) on a
  **midnight** `#06080F` ground. Blue is a rare, quiet accent — it never competes
  with orange.
- **Type** — **Oswald 700** shouts the headlines, **Barlow** speaks the body,
  **JetBrains Mono** handles the technical tells.
- **Voice** — plain, confident, on the side of the little guy. We don't sell
  "solutions" — we fix the tech slowing down your business.
