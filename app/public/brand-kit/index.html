The spirit

The little guy who moves the big guy.

A tugboat is small, stout, and unglamorous — and it pulls container ships ten thousand times its size through the busiest harbor on earth. That's the whole idea. Little Fight NYC is the tugboat for the small business: we fix the tech slowing you down so you can pull weight far above your size, against corporations with a hundred times your budget. The mark isn't decoration. It's the promise.

Reverses on any ground

One mark. One color, set once in CSS. It knocks out clean on midnight, on orange, on paper — even on a busy photo with a scrim. Tap a ground → the boat never breaks.

✓ Do
  • Give it air — clear space ≥ the height of the smokestack on all sides.
  • Minimum size 20px (favicon), 32px anywhere else.
  • Mark in white, ink, or orange only. "NYC" tag carries the orange.
✕ Don't
  • Recolor, gradient, shadow, outline, rotate, or stretch the boat.
  • Set the wordmark in anything but Oswald 700.
  • Put the mark on a busy photo without a solid or scrimmed ground.
