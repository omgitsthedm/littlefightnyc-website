Brand Kit v2.0

Make it clear. Keep it current.

Clarity, accessibility, and governance are part of the brand, not a cleanup pass.

Access is a brand rule

  • Use a 16px absolute type floor. Body copy normally sits at 18px to 20px.
  • Keep touch targets at least 44px and keyboard focus clearly visible.
  • Never rely on color alone to communicate state.
  • Write alt text for meaning, not visual decoration.
  • Allow long translations, CJK text, long URLs, and 200 percent zoom.
  • Respect reduced motion and keep the complete page available without JavaScript.
