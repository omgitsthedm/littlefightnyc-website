Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

By David Marsh, Little Fight NYC · Reviewed for current site operations and hosting practices.

Overview

Little Fight NYC ("we," "us," or "our") respects visitor privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when visitors browse the site or use company services.

Information We Collect

We collect details provided directly, such as when visitors:

  • Fill out a contact form
  • Request our services
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Contact us via email or phone

How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Deliver the services you requested
  • Send you updates about our services (if you opted in)
  • Improve our website and services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal records to third parties. We may share certain details with trusted service providers who assist in operating the website and conducting business, as long as those parties agree to keep this data confidential.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal records against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. The website is served over HTTPS with modern TLS encryption, and we regularly review the hosting environment and third-party integrations for security vulnerabilities. Access to stored personal data is restricted to authorized personnel who need it to fulfill service requests.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how visitors interact with our site. We use essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly, such as remembering your preferences. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising. If our site integrates analytics tools, they collect anonymized usage data such as pages visited and session duration to help us improve site performance. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences at any time.

Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with external services such as form processors, analytics platforms, or embedded content providers. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. When we use third-party tools to process data on our behalf, we select providers that maintain strong data protection standards and limit their use of your information to the purposes we specify.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
