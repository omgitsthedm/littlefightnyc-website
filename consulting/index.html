By David Marsh · Published May 6, 2026

Most owners come to us with a hunch — "we’re paying too much for software" or "the website is fine but leads still die." A consulting walkthrough turns the hunch into a real next move. The first hour is free — we sit with you, list every tool you pay for, watch how staff actually uses them, and find where time and money leak. If a deeper audit makes sense, we scope it by depth: a focused walkthrough is shorter and lighter, a full audit with a written 5–10 page fight plan goes deeper. The plan is yours regardless of who does the work. No upsell. No retainer trap. No surprise invoices.

Is this for you?

When the Walkthrough makes sense.

Yes — book it

  • You’re paying $300+/month for software and don’t know which is dead weight
  • You feel a workflow is broken but can’t name the fix
  • You’ve been burned by an agency or freelancer and want a second opinion before spending again
  • You want a written plan you can shop around or do yourself
  • You’d rather pay for an hour of honest advice than guess and waste thousands on the wrong fix

No — skip it

  • You already know exactly what you want built — just hire us for that
  • You want free advice (start with the Fit Check)
  • You need a fix today, not a 30-day plan
  • You won’t actually act on the plan when delivered

What’s in the plan

A real document, not vague advice.

1. Software inventory

Every subscription, what it costs, what it does, who uses it, and whether it earns its bill.

2. Workflow map

How a customer actually moves from "find you" to "pay you" to "come back." Where time leaks.

3. Money-leak ranking

The biggest, second-biggest, third-biggest places cash and customer-attention are escaping.

4. Prioritized action list

What to fix first — the move that pays for itself fastest. Then second, third, fourth.

5. Specific tool calls

"Replace X with Y, here’s why, here’s the cost diff." Real names. Real numbers.

6. DIY vs. hire-help split

Honest read on which fixes you can do yourself, and which need a real builder.

Owner FAQ

Questions owners ask first.

What is a Little Fight consulting walkthrough?

A 30-day audit. We come into your business, map every tool, workflow, software bill, and broken handoff. Then we write a fight plan: what to keep, what to connect, what to replace, and what to build. You leave with a real document, not vague advice.

How much does it cost?

The first hour is free — tell us what feels broken or expensive and we’ll point you at the smallest useful next move. Longer engagements scope by depth: a focused walkthrough is shorter and lighter, a full audit with a written 5–10 page fight plan goes deeper. We always quote honestly before you commit. No hidden fees, no upsell pressure.

Why pay for a plan instead of just hiring you for the work?

Two reasons. One: the plan is yours regardless of who does the work. You can give it to another developer or do parts yourself. Two: most owners don’t actually know which fix matters most. The plan picks the one that pays for itself fastest.

Can you just give me advice without the audit?

Free advice is what the Fit Check is for. The walkthrough is a real engagement with a deliverable. If you don’t need a written plan, you don’t need this — start with the Fit Check.

What’s in the written fight plan?

Software subscription list with what it costs and whether it earns it. Workflow map showing where time leaks. Prioritized action list — biggest money/time leak first. Specific tool recommendations. Honest assessment of which fixes you can do yourself vs. need help with.

Do you work with shops outside NYC?

NYC and surrounding areas get on-site. Anywhere else in the US gets a video walkthrough at the same price. The plan format is identical.
