Are AI tools worth it for a small business?

Only when they save real work, not when they add another tab.

📞 Call (646) 360-0318 💬 Text us ✉️ Email us 📝 Contact form
By David Marsh · Published May 6, 2026

Direct answer

AI tools can be useful for drafting replies, summarizing leads, sorting intake, planning content, and reducing admin work. They are not worth it when nobody owns the workflow, outputs are not reviewed, or the tool creates another monthly bill without a clear job.

  • What exact task should AI reduce?
  • Who reviews the output?
  • Where does the AI result go next?
  • Does it save staff time every week?
  • Can the business avoid sensitive data exposure?
