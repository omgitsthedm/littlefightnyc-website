POS keeps freezing?

Do not wait until the line is staring at you.

Start HereCall if it is broken
By David Marsh · Published May 6, 2026

Direct answer

If a POS keeps freezing, check the network, device health, software updates, payment reader, printer connection, and whether the issue happens at certain times or stations. If it affects customers or payments right now, treat it as urgent support first and a deeper setup review second.

  • Does it happen on one device or every station?
  • Does Wi-Fi drop at the same time?
  • Are printers or card readers involved?
  • Did an update or hardware change happen recently?
  • Is the team using workarounds during rush?
