Website costs

What should a small business website cost in NYC?

Enough to make customers trust you. Not enough to fund someone else's sales deck.

Call (646) 360-0318 Text us Email us Contact form
By David Marsh · Published May 6, 2026

Direct answer

$5,000–$15,000 from a NYC agency, $200–$500/year DIY. Independent shops (like Little Fight) come in around half the agency rate for the same work.

A NYC small business website typically runs $5,000–$15,000 from a NYC agency with account managers and design rounds, or $200–$500/year if you build it yourself on Squarespace, Webflow, or Wix. A small independent shop (like Little Fight) usually comes in around half the agency rate — we don’t post a fixed price because every project scopes differently, but our clients save 50% on average compared to NYC agency quotes for the same work. The right price depends on whether you need it to actually convert customers or just exist online. Watch out for monthly traps: $40–$80/month "platform fees" that add up to $2,000+ over 5 years.

  • $5,000–$15,000: typical NYC agency price for a small business site
  • $200–$500/year: DIY on Squarespace, Webflow, or Wix templates
  • Independent shops (like Little Fight) usually scope ~50% below agency rates for equivalent work
  • Watch for monthly traps: $40–$80/mo "platform fees" cost $2,000+ over 5 years
  • Live in 14 days is realistic for a focused 5–7 page site — agencies typically quote 8–12 weeks for the same scope
