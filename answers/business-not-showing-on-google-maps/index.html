Google help

Not showing on Google Maps?

Usually it is not one mystery. It is a trust trail Google cannot read clearly.

Start HereCall if it is broken
By David Marsh · Published May 6, 2026

Direct answer

If your business is not showing on Google Maps, check whether the Google Business Profile is verified, the category is right, the address or service area is accurate, reviews are active, and the website clearly supports the services and neighborhood you want to rank for.

  • Confirm the profile is verified and not suspended.
  • Check primary and secondary categories.
  • Match name, phone, website, and service area across the web.
  • Add service pages that match real customer searches.
  • Keep reviews coming in naturally and consistently.
