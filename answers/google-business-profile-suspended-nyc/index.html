Google profile suspended?

Do not panic-click through it. Get the facts clean first.

Start HereCall if it is broken
By David Marsh · Published May 6, 2026

Direct answer

If a Google Business Profile is suspended, check the suspension reason, business name, category, address or service area, website match, phone, documents, and whether any recent edit triggered the issue. A rushed appeal with messy information can make recovery harder.

  • Was the profile recently edited?
  • Does the business name match real-world signage and website?
  • Is the address or service area accurate?
  • Are categories appropriate?
  • Do you have documents or proof ready?
