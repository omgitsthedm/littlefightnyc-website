When each wins

Honest read on which is right for you.

Pick GEEK-AID if:

  • You have 10+ employees with consistent IT load
  • You want a predictable $89.95/employee monthly bill
  • You need 24/7 human emergency response
  • You don’t need website / custom software work alongside IT
  • You’re fine with rotating helpdesk technicians

Pick Little Fight if:

  • You’re owner-operated, 1–25 staff
  • You want one human who knows your whole setup
  • You need websites, custom systems, OR consulting alongside IT
  • You only have 1–3 IT calls per month and don’t need unlimited
  • You’d rather have an AI phone agent + David than a rotating helpdesk

Owner FAQ

Questions owners ask first.

Should a NYC small business pick GEEK-AID or Little Fight for IT support?

GEEK-AID wins for businesses that want unlimited monthly IT support at a fixed per-employee rate ($89.95/employee/mo), need 24/7 human emergency response, and don’t care about working with a founder. Little Fight wins for owners who want one human who knows their setup, who also need websites or custom systems alongside IT, who value pricing transparency, and who like the AI phone agent + business-hours response model.

What does GEEK-AID actually cost?

GEEK-AID’s monthly plan is $89.95 per employee per month for unlimited on-site support. One-off help is $295 for the first hour, then $139/hour after. Their model favors larger employee counts on the monthly plan.

What does Little Fight charge?

$200/hour on-location after a proper intake. We’re always fair for small business — even if our pricing doesn’t work for your situation, reach out anyway. If we can’t help, we’ll let you know who can for less.

Why doesn’t Little Fight have a monthly retainer?

We’re considering one. The challenge: monthly unlimited only makes sense if a shop actually generates enough IT issues to justify it. Most NYC small businesses we serve have 1–3 IT calls per month, which costs less hourly than a $89.95/employee monthly. We’d rather you only pay for what you need. If a monthly plan is right for your shop, we’ll talk through it during the intake.

Does Little Fight offer 24/7 emergency response like GEEK-AID?

GEEK-AID offers 24/7 human emergency response — they have the staff for it. Little Fight offers 2-hour response during NYC business hours (9am-9pm Eastern), plus a 24-hour live AI phone agent that captures the issue and routes urgent calls to David at any hour. For most NYC small business issues that aren’t life-or-death, that combination is enough. For mission-critical 24/7 enterprise IT, GEEK-AID or a larger MSP is the right call.

Can GEEK-AID build my website?

No. GEEK-AID is IT-only. Little Fight handles websites, custom systems, consulting, and IT under one roof — useful when something breaks across all of them at once.
