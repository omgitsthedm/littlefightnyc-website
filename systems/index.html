Owner FAQ

Questions owners ask first.

What is a custom business system?

Software built for how your shop actually runs. Instead of paying monthly for five generic apps that don’t talk to each other, we build one tool that handles your specific workflow — bookings, inventory, customer follow-up, dashboards, whatever you need.

How long does it take to build?

First working version in 30 days. We start with the smallest useful piece you can use day one, then expand from there based on what’s actually working.

How much does a custom system cost?

From $4,000 for a focused build. Larger multi-feature systems run $8,000–$15,000+ depending on scope. Monthly hosting and care is typically $50–$200/mo. Almost always cheaper than the SaaS stack it replaces over 18 months.

When does custom beat off-the-shelf software?

When the monthly tool costs more than $200/month and your staff still has to work around it. When you’ve outgrown a generic app but the next tier costs 5× more. When two or three apps need to share data and don’t. When the workflow is unique enough that no SaaS fits.

What if I don’t know exactly what I need?

That’s what the Fit Check is for. We talk for 60 seconds, you tell us what’s broken or expensive, we tell you whether a custom system makes sense or if a smaller fix would do.

Do you work with what we already use?

Yes. Square, Toast, Shopify, Squarespace, Webflow, Stripe, Google Workspace, anything with an API. We connect what works, replace what drains.
