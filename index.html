Questions? We've got answers.

No tech-speak. No runaround. Just straight talk from people who fix this stuff every day.

How fast can you get to my business?+
We aim for 2-hour response time in Manhattan on calls before 5pm. Most of the time, we're faster. We've shown up in 45 minutes during lunch rush POS crashes. For emergencies, text or call us and we'll prioritize you.
How much does IT support cost?+
Your first hour of consulting is completely free. After that, hourly rates start at $150 for standard support. We also offer fixed-price projects and monthly retainers. Call (646) 360-0318 and we'll give you a real quote — no surprises.
Do you support Macs and PCs?+
Both. We fix Macs, PCs, iPhones, iPads, printers, networks, POS systems, websites, and anything else getting in your way. If it plugs in, connects to Wi-Fi, or has a screen — we can help.
Can you help businesses outside NYC?+
Absolutely. We do on-site visits throughout Manhattan, but we support businesses nationwide via remote support — screen sharing, video calls, VPN setup, cloud migrations, website design, and SEO work from anywhere in the US.
What if you can't fix the problem?+
No Fix, No Fee. If we can't solve it, you don't pay for our time. We're confident in what we do, and we won't waste your money on things that won't work. That's the Little Fight promise.
Why is it called "Little Fight"?+
Because small businesses are in a fight every day — against rising rents, big corporations, and outdated tech. We're the scrappy team in your corner. We believe in NYC, we believe in small business, and we're here to help you win.
