Pricing principle

The goal is the smallest useful move.

Sometimes the right move is a new build. Sometimes it is a small repair, removing a duplicate subscription, or making one lead path reliable. The Fit Check exists so the recommendation has a reason.

We price around the thing costing the most money, customers, time, or trust. A website project may include form routing or local search because those pieces decide whether the website actually pays off. A savings project may stay small if the highest-value change is one cleaner intake path, one owner view, or removing duplicate tools.
