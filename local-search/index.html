Local intent

Google should understand you as well as it understands the chain.

People search when they need something now. Your business has to be clear enough for Google to show and trustworthy enough for someone to choose.

In New York, the decision happens fast. The profile must be accurate, the page must answer, and the next step must be obvious.

  • The Google profile is incomplete or wrong.
  • The site has one vague page instead of clear service pages.
  • Reviews do not answer the buyer's real worries.
  • Customers ask the same questions before they call.
  • Nobody knows which searches are turning into leads.
