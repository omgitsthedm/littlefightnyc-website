Owner FAQ

Questions owners ask first.

What does Wix actually cost over 5 years?

Wix Light is $17/month, Core $29/month, Business $36/month, Business Elite $159/month. Over 5 years that’s $1,020 (Light) to $9,540 (Elite). Plus your time learning the editor (most owners spend 30–50 hours), plus paid apps for booking/SEO/email/etc. Real total typically lands $2,500–$6,000 over 5 years for a working setup.

Does Google penalize Wix websites?

Google doesn’t penalize Wix specifically, but Wix template sites consistently underperform on Core Web Vitals (load speed), have generic schema, and load excessive JavaScript that hurts Largest Contentful Paint. Custom sites built for performance routinely outrank template Wix sites for the same keywords. The platform doesn’t get penalized — your specific site does, because templates inherit the slow common code.

How much faster is a custom site vs Wix?

Custom hand-coded sites typically load 2–4× faster than equivalent Wix template sites. Lighthouse Performance score: custom sites in the 95–100 range, Wix templates often in the 50–75 range. That gap matters because Google ranks faster sites higher and visitors bounce on slow ones.

You don’t have to pay monthly for a website?

Correct. A custom-built website is yours outright after launch. You pay a one-time build fee, then $25/month for hosting (or less if you self-host). No platform tax. No "your subscription will increase 20% next year" emails. Over 5 years, the savings vs Wix’s monthly model is typically $500–$1,500+.

Is Wix ever the right answer for a NYC small business?

Yes — if you have one offering, no integrations beyond what Wix App Market includes, time to learn the editor, and the site doesn’t have to convert hard. We’ll tell you that honestly. The first hour of consulting is free; we’ll point you at Wix if that’s the right call.
