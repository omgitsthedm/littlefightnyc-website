Fit map

What usually has to line up for retail and e-commerce businesses.

For retail and e-commerce teams, the system has to reconcile what people see online with what staff know in the shop. Product data, pickup options, POS inventory, email capture, return questions, and reporting all need a practical owner view.

A stronger platform is not automatically the answer. Sometimes the right move is cleaner product structure, better collection pages, simpler pickup logic, or a owner view that makes sales and demand signals visible.
