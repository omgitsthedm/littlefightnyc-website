The issue

If the team still uses spreadsheets, notes, and memory, the expensive platform is not doing its job.

You may not need a bigger app. You may need one cleaner place for leads, bookings, requests, follow-up, and the work everyone keeps copying by hand.

Decision rule

Use subscriptions when they help. Stop feeding them when they do not.

Keep the monthly tool when

  • Staff actually use it.
  • It saves more time than it costs.
  • It handles something standard well.
  • It protects trust, payments, or compliance.

Consider a smaller custom fix when

  • The platform is expensive and underused.
  • The business still runs through spreadsheets.
  • Data lives in too many places.
  • Staff are working around the tool every day.
