Is this for you?

Who Bullpen IT support fits.

Yes — call us

  • You’re a NYC business under 50 staff — shop, salon, restaurant, studio, practice, office
  • Something breaks and the corporate IT helpdesk model doesn’t fit (too slow, too expensive, too generic)
  • You want one human who knows your setup, not a ticket queue
  • You’d rather pay a fair retainer than wait an hour for a $300 emergency callout
  • Your tech is a mix of Apple, Square, Wi-Fi, printers, email, websites — it doesn’t fit one vendor’s box

No — we’re not a fit

  • You have 50+ staff and need a real managed services provider with 24/7 SOC
  • You’re running enterprise infrastructure (Active Directory, Exchange Server, datacenter)
  • You want a $99/month "unlimited" support contract — nobody actually delivers that
  • The problem is software development, not infrastructure (try /systems/)

What we fix

Real things, real time-to-fix.

POS & payments

Square, Toast, Clover, iPad registers, Stripe, card readers, receipt printers, payment-gateway issues. Most fixes 1-2 hours.

Wi-Fi & networks

Mesh setup, guest Wi-Fi separation, dropouts, bandwidth issues, VLANs, router/switch reconfiguration. Eero, Ubiquiti, Cisco, you name it.

Computers & devices

Mac, PC, iPad, iPhone setup, repair, deployment. Backup, recovery, encryption, MDM. Lost laptop? We get you running same-day.

Email & domain

Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, DNS, domain transfers, deliverability fixes (when emails stop arriving), MX records, SPF/DKIM/DMARC.

Smart-home & business

Smart locks, security cameras, thermostats, integrated AV. Set up so the owner can use them, not the genius who installed them.

The "I don’t even know what to call this"

Half of IT support is the messy in-between. Two apps that don’t talk. A vendor that disappeared. A bill nobody can identify. We figure it out.

Owner FAQ

Questions owners ask first.

What does Little Fight IT support cover?

POS systems, Wi-Fi, networks, computers (Mac and PC), email, printers, smart home/business setup, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, domain/DNS, and the things in between that don’t fit one vendor’s box.

What’s the 2-hour response promise?

For NYC clients during business hours (9am-7pm ET), we respond to urgent issues within 2 hours. If we don’t, that hour is free. Off-hours fixes still happen but the 2-hour clock is business hours.

How much does NYC IT support cost?

$150/hour for one-off fixes. $500/month retainer for ongoing care (covers ~4 hours, urgent calls included). For most NYC small businesses, the retainer pays for itself in one prevented disaster.

Do you do on-site visits?

Yes. Manhattan and most outer borough locations get on-site within 2 hours. Remote-first when it’ll work, on-site when it won’t.

What if I just have a one-time problem?

Call (646) 360-0318 or start a Fit Check. We bill hourly with no minimum. Most one-time issues take 1-3 hours.

Do you support Apple, Windows, or both?

Both. Most NYC shops run Apple devices with maybe one Windows machine for accounting. We handle the mix.

If customers, payments, bookings, or staff are blocked — that’s urgent. We answer in under 2 hours during business hours.
