Is this for you?

Who Bullpen IT support fits.

Yes — call us

  • You’re a NYC business under 50 staff — shop, salon, restaurant, studio, practice, office
  • Something breaks and the corporate IT helpdesk model doesn’t fit (too slow, too expensive, too generic)
  • You want one human who knows your setup, not a ticket queue
  • You’d rather pay a fair retainer than wait an hour for a $300 emergency callout
  • Your tech is a mix of Apple, Square, Wi-Fi, printers, email, websites — it doesn’t fit one vendor’s box

No — we’re not a fit

  • You have 50+ staff and need a real managed services provider with 24/7 SOC
  • You’re running enterprise infrastructure (Active Directory, Exchange Server, datacenter)
  • You want a $99/month "unlimited" support contract — nobody actually delivers that
  • The problem is software development, not infrastructure (try /systems/)

What we fix

Real things, real time-to-fix.

POS & payments

Square, Toast, Clover, iPad registers, Stripe, card readers, receipt printers, payment-gateway issues. Most fixes 1-2 hours.

Wi-Fi & networks

Mesh setup, guest Wi-Fi separation, dropouts, bandwidth issues, VLANs, router/switch reconfiguration. Eero, Ubiquiti, Cisco, you name it.

Computers & devices

Mac, PC, iPad, iPhone setup, repair, deployment. Backup, recovery, encryption, MDM. Lost laptop? We get you running same-day.

Email & domain

Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, DNS, domain transfers, deliverability fixes (when emails stop arriving), MX records, SPF/DKIM/DMARC.

Smart-home & business

Smart locks, security cameras, thermostats, integrated AV. Set up so the owner can use them, not the genius who installed them.
