What to Expect When You Reach Out

We aim to respond to every inquiry within one business day, usually sooner. When you contact us, you will hear back from a real person who understands small business technology, not a chatbot or an outsourced call center. Our initial conversations are always free and pressure-free. We listen first, then recommend only what makes sense for your situation and budget.

Where do you provide service?

We provide on-site IT support and technology services throughout all five boroughs of New York City, with most of our in-person work concentrated in Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. For website design, SEO, and digital consulting, we work with clients anywhere in the United States remotely.

What if I am not sure what I need?

That is perfectly fine and actually very common. Many of our clients start with a general question like "my website is not showing up on Google" or "I need help choosing a POS system." We will help you figure out the right next step without pushing unnecessary services. Just describe what is going on and we will take it from there.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. In addition to one-time projects, we offer ongoing support plans for businesses that want a reliable technology partner on call. Whether you need monthly website maintenance, regular IT check-ins, or help as issues arise, we can set up a plan that fits your needs and keeps things running smoothly long term.
