Booking platform tier ladder

Pick the tier that fits the chair count.

Solo to 5 stylists

Square Appointments ($0/mo + 2.6% per transaction) or GlossGenius ($24/mo flat). Both handle online booking, deposits, reminders, basic client records, and Instagram booking links.

Mindbody ($129+/mo) or Momence ($150+/mo). Built for class scheduling, packages, and large client bases.

Salon owner FAQ

Questions NYC salon owners ask first.

What’s the best booking software for a NYC salon?

Solo to 5 stylists: Square Appointments ($0/mo + 2.6% transaction fee) or GlossGenius ($24/mo flat). 5+ stylists with memberships and packages: Boulevard ($175+/mo per location). Wellness/spa with classes: Mindbody ($129+/mo). Most NYC salons we audit are paying for a tier they don’t need.

How do NYC salons handle no-shows?

Deposits at booking ($25–$50 typical), 24–48 hour cancellation rules, automated reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before, and a no-show fee equal to the deposit. Square Appointments and GlossGenius both handle this. Most NYC salons lose 10–20% of revenue to no-shows; deposits cut it to under 5%.

What does a NYC salon website cost?

Independent shops (like Little Fight) come in around $2,000–$5,000 for a real working salon site: services with prices, online booking embed, stylist profiles, before/after gallery, mobile-first. NYC agencies charge $5,000–$15,000+. DIY platforms run $200–$500/year but take 30+ hours of your time.

How do you compete with Sephora for product sales?

You probably can’t on price. You can on relationship: track which products each client uses, automate reminders when they’re due to repurchase, sell as upsell at checkout in-shop. The math: 30 clients × $20 product profit × 1 visit/month = $600/mo extra. Doesn’t sound like much until you realize it’s almost a chair-cost paid for.

What about Drybar or chain salon competition?

You win on the relationship and the result, not on tech. But the tech matters: a clunky booking flow, missed reminders, or a website that doesn’t show your work loses you to the chain that has those buttoned up. We bring the chain’s polish to your shop’s tech.
