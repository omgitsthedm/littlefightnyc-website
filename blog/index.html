What belongs here

Field notes are for decisions, not filler.

A useful note should help an owner make a better call before money moves: whether to rebuild a website, choose Square or Toast, clean up a Google profile, connect forms to a CRM, or stop paying for software that only solves half the workflow. The notes stay close to actual business pressure: rent, staffing, handoffs, search behavior, booking flow, follow-up, and the cost of keeping a fragile setup alive.
